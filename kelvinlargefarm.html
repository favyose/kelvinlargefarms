About Us

What you need to know?

At KELVIN LARGE's FARM, we pride ourselves on our heritage of generations of expertise in poultry farming. We evolved into a thriving enterprise, driven by a shared love for poultry and a commitment to sustainable and ethical farming practices.
We believe that great products start with happy, healthy birds. That's why we've built our farm upon principles of ethical and sustainable farming practices, ensuring that our chickens enjoy the best possible living conditions and nutrition.
From the moment our eggs are laid to the day our broilers reach your table, we prioritize quality every step of the way. Our eggs are fresh and clean. We are delighted to thank you for choosing KELVIN LARGE's FARM.

Our Vision

What we look up to !

Our mission is simple: to raise healthy, happy birds while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental stewardship. From our spacious, meticulously maintained coops to our carefully curated feed and healthcare regimens, every aspect of our operation is designed to ensure the well-being of our feathered friends.
